Sound Money of the 21st Century

Over the coming decades we see an accelerating trend of people who want to use gold as a means to store value against inflation and to use gold for mainstream payments between individuals and institutions. Currently there is a small core of people who recognize the value of gold as a store of value and hedge against political risk, and we aim to provide a vastly superior payment system to them while positioning ourselves as the leader in the movement toward gold-based money systems.

How it Works

In each participating Texas county a secure warehouse is maintained which houses gold, silver, and copper coins minted by the Republic. The coins are then registered in the distributed digital Ripple ledger, enabling a public digital representation of the coin that can be provably owned using mathematical keys. Ownership of the ditigal coin representation can then be transferred to any other user with a mathematical key.
